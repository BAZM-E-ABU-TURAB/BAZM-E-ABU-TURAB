BAZM-E-ABU TURAB AS EDUCATIONAL AND SOCIAL WELFARE SOCIETY

Vision

An empowered, educated, and self-reliant society where every individual, regardless of background, thrives with dignity, health, and social harmony.

Mission

To design and deliver holistic, inclusive, and sustainable programs in education, healthcare, and social welfare; to empower marginalized groups through skill development and advocacy; and to cultivate spiritual harmony and environmental responsibility for the collective betterment of communities in and around Khuldabad.

Objectives

  • Establish multilingual educational institutions and provide scholarships.
  • Organize medical camps, distribute free medicines, and raise health awareness.
  • Support widows, orphans, the homeless, and vulnerable families with basic needs.
  • Empower women through SHGs and financial literacy.
  • Promote youth leadership, legal awareness, and cultural unity.
  • Preserve heritage, foster interfaith harmony, and protect the environment.
  • Ensure ethical, transparent governance and mobilize resources sustainably.
